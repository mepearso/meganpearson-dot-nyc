WORK

THE LIST

a bucket list app for travel destinations

Using the Panoramio and Google Places APIs, I created a visual bucket list app for users to save locations they want to visit.

Users can edit and save field notes and timeframes (without page reload) for each location and see a customized google map of all of their saved places.

LOVINGLY MADE WITH: RUBY ON RAILS, BACKBONE, GOOGLE PLACES API, PANORAMIO API, GOOGLE MAPS & CUSTOM RESPONSIVE CSS

ABSURD NYC

tongue-in-cheek look at NYC and her occupants

On this group project, we developed an app that pokes a bit of fun at New York City and her residents. Users can choose a neighborhood they want to hang out in and are then assigned a stereotyped, curated day plan based on their choice.

Using the Instagram and Yelp APIs, we customized the day plan activities with photos and restaurant & bar reviews.

LOVINGLY MADE WITH: RUBY ON RAILS, INSTAGRAM & YELP APIS, JQUERY & CUSTOM RESPONSIVE CSS

LOOK UP

cosmos and astrological themed wiki

I designed this wiki to allow users to search for relevant information about their birthdays. Specifically, a user can enter their birth month and day and discover their zodiac, the largest star in their zodiological constellation, and the planet associated with their birth month.

Users can create and edit articles, using Markdown, and will be automatically emailed, via the Mailgun API, when an article they've written has been edited.

I created all of the graphics with a mixture of hand-drawing, Photoshop & Illustrator.

LOVINGLY MADE WITH: NODE.JS, EXPRESS, JQUERY, MAILGUN API, MARKED, CUSTOM RESPONSIVE CSS, PHOTOSHOP & ILLUSTRATOR

TIC TAC GOAT

tic tac toe with a goat

In this classic children's game, I built a simple computer opponent to play a random move. More importantly, I incorporated a screaming goat to alert players when they've made an incorrect move (going when it's not their turn or moving to a space that's already been selected).

LOVINGLY MADE WITH: JQUERY, PHOTO OF GOAT, SOUND CLIP OF SCREAMING GOAT & CUSTOM RESPONSIVE CSS
